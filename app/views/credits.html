This database contains routing information for the American Radio Relay League National Traffic System in New York State.

The routing tables were originally compiled by Carl R. Preddy KU2N and Edward Krymowski NE2W in the New York State Traffic Routing Guide in 1986.

The guide was scanned and processed by Art DeBruycker W2YGW in 2005.

Nick Castellano N2QZ started with Art's spreadsheet in 2008, cleaned up hundreds of OCR errors, updated the data to bring it closer to current reality, and created this web search form.

Vince Harzewski N2JRS provided significant corrections in December 2020.

Andy Harmon-Kimball W2ZXN / WRPI357 provided updated information concerning NNY/CNY/OCT in April 2022 and May 2023.
